Meet The Author

Creative expression transforms culture like nothing else. From an early age, creating stories was a way for me to unwind and process the world around me. After I lost my mom writing became even more of a refuge. As I have gotten older I have realized not only its cathartic properties, for me personally, but also the ability to teach I connect and uncover mindsets that impact our culture. As you read I hope you learn something about yourself and gain a deeper understanding of where we all live.

Now On Wattpad

This is the first book in the Shadow River series. These stories chronicle the lives of the inhabitants of the fictional southern Alberta town of Shadow River. The stories span 3 generations. 

Scarlett Graham is the only remaining member of her family, they are all dead. Both of her parents and grandmother died in a car accident when she was a baby and her grandfather who raised her is gone now too. His death has left her feeling anchorless and afraid.

Elijah River Stone is the only son of a murdered human trafficker. He has made it his life purpose to right the wrongs that his family has caused through the generations. He meets Scarlett while conducting a job interview to hire a physical education and health teacher for the school he is the principal of. 

 Will they be able to walk through the darkness and loss of the past and discover in each other where the light lives?

Coming Soon

The second book in the Shadow River series.

Ainsley meets Sky while she is trespassing on the land he just purchased. Even though they are nearly perfect strangers they feel as though they have known each other their entire lives.

Ainsley is the 15 year old daughter of the mayor of Shadow River her father is cautious and fearful that his daughter will repeat the mistakes of his past and is very protective. 

Sky the son of a wealthy land owner who broke away from the control of his family. He has bought the property neighboring the acreage Ainsley has lived on all her life. He knows she is too young for the kind of commitment he wants but is he willing to wait? Will her father’s fear keep her from the man that will become the love of her life?

In Development

This book is the first instalment of The Portal Tree Chronicles. An augmented reality graphics novel series complete with a digital marketplace, interactive three dimensional town, performing arts center, and in game transportation network. The whole series is set in the fictional town on the central west coast of British Columbia called Oak Falls.

In this story we meet a young woman named Alisa. She meets and ends up being partners with a dancer named Freddy. Their journey is narrated by Michael a time travelling prince who comes to earth from a utopian kingdom.

Since it is augmented reality all the performances, clothing, food, hair, makeup and other creative processes will be a digital recreation of actual physical products and services that people will view, connect with and purchase in a fully interactive 3D marketplace.
